About Us

Professional Corporate Security Services, Inc. is a licensed, bonded, and fully insured security agency providing services in New Jerseyand New York. PCSS carries a one million dollar general liability policy. PCSS Security is the premier nightclub security provider in New York . We are a major security service provider of armed and unarmed officers in the personal, residential, and commercial sectors as well as in event services, executive protection, and corporate services.

Through our consulting division, we are able to provide our clients with confidential surveys to reduce liability. Our security personnel are licensed, bonded, and insured by the State of New Jersey, New York, and Florida. PCSS is not only committed to safeguarding and protecting, but also, to reducing liability as much as possible. Our security personnel are managed by expert security professionals and former law enforcement, military, and treasury department members.

Effective and Creative Management

We take pride in knowing that the fundamental key to our success derives from our highly effective and creative management policies. Our management staff posses the ability to lead and formulate a balanced company environment where each of our employee’s internal motivation can flourish. We understand that in order to provide total quality service to all of our clients, we must provide total quality support to our employees.

As a leading security service provider, our personnel are highly trained, supervised, and specifically selected for each individual client’s needs. The nature of our job challenges us to ensure our commitments to our clients. These challenges have motivated us to successfully safeguard, protect, and reduce liability on every assignment to date.

Our Philosophy

Our philosphy at PCSS is simple: professionalism in every aspect. Our philosophy is carried out through every action we conduct. Our size, experienced management, and a commitment to total customer satisfaction ensures PCSS an excellent position in the market. Our four pillars of an astounding security service are:

  • Bring a degree of experience, dedication, and professionalism to the security industry

  • Bring new innovative ideas to the table, therefore assuring our clients the latest techniques in security performance

  • Work endlessly to build a lasting, and ethical relationship which is beneficial to our clients security and growth

  • Provide tailored services to the unique operating conditions and demographic of our clients site
